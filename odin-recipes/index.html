First of all... What is cooking?

Cooking is the process of preparing food by applying heat to ingredients in order to transform them into a form that is safe to eat, palatable, and enjoyable. This process can involve a variety of techniques, including boiling, baking, frying, grilling, roasting, sautéing, steaming, and more.

Cooking involves a combination of science, art, and creativity, as well as knowledge of different ingredients, their properties, and how they interact with one another. It can also involve cultural and personal preferences, as different cuisines have their own unique flavor profiles, ingredients, and cooking methods.

Cooking can be a fulfilling and enjoyable activity, as it can allow people to create and experiment with different flavors, textures, and presentations. It is also an essential life skill that enables people to nourish themselves and others with delicious and healthy meals.
